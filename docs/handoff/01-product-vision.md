# Product Vision

LUCID Hub should become a trusted European platform where local digital relationships can happen in one place without collapsing into a chaotic all-in-one app.

The product vision is a modular super-app for:

- Customer and business communication.
- Local discovery.
- Communities and clubs.
- Creator and business channels.
- Bookings and service catalogs.
- Coupons and loyalty.
- Mini-services through reviewed mini-apps.
- Commerce and regulated payment-provider integrations later.

The product should feel European in its trust posture: privacy-by-design, clear consent, transparent moderation, minimal data collection, and no dark patterns.

The MVP should prove that a business or creator can create a verified presence, communicate with customers, publish updates, manage bookings, and issue rewards. Customers should be able to discover trusted local services, follow channels, join communities, and interact with businesses from one coherent account.

Non-goals for MVP:

- No LUCID-owned wallet.
- No peer-to-peer money transfer.
- No card storage.
- No bank credential storage.
- No custom production cryptography.
- No AI training on private chats.
- No public admin panel without access control.
