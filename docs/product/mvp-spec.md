# MVP Spec

## Product Positioning

LUCID Hub is a European trust layer for local digital life. It brings together verified local business profiles, customer communication, discovery, communities, channels, bookings, coupons, and commerce primitives while preserving privacy-by-design.

## Primary Audience

- Small European businesses and venues.
- Creators, clubs, and local communities.
- Customers who want trusted local discovery, bookings, loyalty, and communication in one place.

## MVP User Jobs

- A business creates a verified profile and publishes updates.
- A customer discovers a trusted local business or creator.
- A customer follows a channel, joins a community, books a service, or redeems a coupon.
- A moderator reviews reports and records actions with appeal support.

## Explicit Non-Scope

- No LUCID-owned wallet.
- No peer-to-peer money transfer.
- No card or bank credential storage.
- No production custom cryptography.
- No AI training on private chats.

## Phase 0 Deliverable

The first implementation pass creates the repository, docs, service shells, shared packages, local infrastructure, CI, Prisma schema skeleton, and smoke tests. Product workflows remain behind future phases and feature flags.
